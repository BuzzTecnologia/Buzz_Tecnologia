Story

The startup Buzz Tecnologia was a society founded during the 2nd semester of 2020 by 7 programming students (a group that met and got together during a training course at the Technical School Basilides de Godoy), a year that saw an opportunity to enter the labor market through the pandemic. In order to develop systems for small and large projects, the startup aims to always value its 4 pillars: organization, comfort, communication and valuing both the final consumer and the team as a whole.

Mission

Develop programs with the automation of processes that satisfy the needs of our customers, ensuring greater productivity and well-being of employees.

Eyesight

To be the largest reference company in systems development in the world, bringing quality, modernity, ease and satisfaction to our customers.

Values

Satisfaction, Valuation and Respect for the client: Our growth is the result of the approval of our clients. We always try to recognize your needs in order to develop a great job.

Excellent communication between employees: Through the mutual exchange of information and experiences between employees, we bring the highest technology to our client making it a differentiating company in the market.

Recognition and reward for employees: We always seek to encourage the personal development of our employees so that they develop their work with the utmost excellence and satisfaction.
